## The Pitch Angle (draft)

> "Lorraine, the management software you're imagining is shaped like every other piece of business software — screens, forms, buttons, dashboards. The problem is that you and your team will spend more time **operating the software** than actually doing your work.
>
> We want to flip it. We build you a system where an AI agent does the operating, and you supervise. You talk to it like a colleague — 'find me three candidates for that warehouse role,' 'send the contracts to the five we shortlisted yesterday,' 'show me which placements are at risk this week.' It does the clicking. You make the decisions.
>
> The screens still exist, but they're for *seeing* what's happening, not for doing the work. The software is symbiotic — built for you and the agent together. This is the next generation. We're already building it for ourselves at blackcode. We'd love to build yours alongside it."
